I graduated cum laude in 2024 with a Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Salerno, specializing in Software Engineering and IT Management.

I am currently a Research Assistant at the Software Engineering Laboratory (SeSa Lab), under the supervision of Prof. Fabio Palomba and Prof. Andrea De Lucia.

My research focuses on Fairness Engineering, Fairness Testing, and Bias Monitoring in Generative AI (GenAI) systems.
