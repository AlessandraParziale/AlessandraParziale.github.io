Research Interests

My research focuses on fairness in Machine Learning systems, addressing both technical and social aspects. During the preparation of my bachelor's and master's theses, I explored the issues of inequalities and biases that these systems can develop over time, with particular attention to their impacts on specific groups of people, exacerbating existing social disparities. From a technical perspective, I analyzed the state of the art and experimented with specific approaches, introducing targeted practices in particular Machine Learning tasks and various application contexts. The main objective was to identify and propose methods to guide the development of Machine Learning models that are both inclusive and high-performing. My research topics include fairness in artificial intelligence systems, bias mitigation in Machine Learning models, and the analysis of practices to promote the responsible development of technologies based on machine learning.
